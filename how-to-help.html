HOW TO HELP

1. MINIMIZE WASTE

One of the main problems facing the ocean environment today is pollution. Sea turtles, dolphins, and other animals often die because they get caught in plastic trash. By minimizing our trash, we avoid contributing to the world’s plastic problem.

2. SAVE ENERGY

Climate change warms oceans and creates an acidic environment that kills corals and other sea life. Burning fossil fuels to create energy contributes to climate change. By turning off lights and appliances when we are not using them, we save energy and decrease demand for energy from fossil fuels.

3. VOLUNTEER AT A BEACH CLEAN-UP

Beaches and seas are littered with plastic and other inorganic debris. By cleaning up these beaches, we can place trash where it belongs–in the landfill. By volunteering at beach clean-ups, we remove dangerous materials from our oceans thus protecting sea life.
